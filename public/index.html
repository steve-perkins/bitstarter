For the first time in human history, we live in a world where most of the people you care about are walking around with Internet-connected computers and GPS tracking devices literally in their pocket. The rise of smart phones open the door to all manner of new possibilities.

When you're out with groups of friends, you can have the freedom to split up, without the hassle of awkwardly calling or texting each other to re-group. Parents can grant kids a greater degree of independence, while feeling safer about it than ever before. Geolocation makes this and so much more easy.

However, these things are not yet ubiquitous, in large part because of privacy concerns. That's where JiffyTracker comes in.

Think of JiffyTracker as a mash-up between Snapchat and geolocation. It gives you all the power of GPS, packed into a powerful mobile app that makes it easy to link up with family and friends. However, the trackers that you create are temporary and disposable, only working for those people you share them with, and only for a limited period of time.

JiffyTracker guards your privacy every step of the way, and stores absolutely no logs or other data about trackers once they expire.

FAQ

What devices will JiffyTracker support?
JiffyTracker will launch with support for Android and iOS. Other devices may follow if there is significant interest. Let us hear from you!
How will JiffyTracker support itself?
JiffyTracker will likely be free and supported by advertising, although respect for privacy is part of our core mission and we will only share anonymous data with advertisers. We may in the future explore paid premium services, such as larger limits on the number of friends and family you can track at once.
What will I get for my support of JiffyTracker?
In addition to ensuring that this service comes to life, all supporters at the level of $10 or above will have their accounts free of advertising for at least the first two years!
